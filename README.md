# Superstore Business Performance Analysis (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes a 4-year Superstore sales dataset (2014–2017) to understand revenue trends, profitability, and operational inefficiencies.

The goal is to identify key business problems and provide data-driven recommendations to improve company performance.

---

## Dataset
Superstore Sales Dataset

Dataset contains:
- 9,994 order records
- 5,009 unique orders
- 793 customers
- 4 regions across the United States
- 3 product categories and 17 sub-categories
- Metrics: Sales, Profit, Quantity, Discount

## Key Business Insights

- Discounts above **20% significantly reduce profitability** and lead to loss-making orders.
- The **Furniture category generates high revenue but very low profit**, indicating pricing or cost issues.
- The **Central region has the lowest profit margin**, mainly due to furniture losses.
- **Technology products produce the highest profit margins**, making them the strongest growth driver.
- A small percentage of customers generate revenue but destroy profit due to excessive discounts.

---

## Strategic Recommendations

1. Cap discounts at **20%** to protect profit margins.
2. Reprice or review loss-making products such as **Tables and Bookcases**.
3. Increase focus on **Technology category** to improve profitability.
4. Improve sales strategy in the **Central region**.
5. Prepare operations for **Q4 demand spikes**, as November and December generate the highest sales.

---


## 💡 Business Impact
- Identified low-profit categories despite high sales
- Highlighted regional performance differences
- Suggested pricing and discount optimization strategies
